# 06 · Your track — pick one outcome, prove it

← [Index](README.md) | Prev: [Setup](05-setup.md) | Next: [Idea Library →](07-idea-library.md)

Three tracks. You pick one at registration; that's the rubric you're scored on. Build a product, agent, or agency crew that proves that outcome by the end of the day. (Full parameter tables: [Virality](scoring-tracks/track-01-virality.md) · [Revenue](scoring-tracks/track-02-revenue.md) · [AI as Agency](scoring-tracks/track-03-ai-as-agency.md).)

| Track | What it is | Root parameter | Base |
|-------|-----------|----------------|------|
| **01 · Virality** | Narrative + distribution. Make something people share, then prove they showed up: impressions, visitors, signups. | Signups / meaningful actions, 25x | 164, overflow uncapped |
| **02 · Revenue** | Signups, product quality, real money moved. 100% live product, no decks. The hardest track — highest cap. | Signups, 20x | 208, overflow uncapped |
| **03 · AI as Agency** | Agents as employees. A team of agents replaces a full human function: marketing, hiring, sales, legal, support, design, engineering. | Working product shipping real output, 20x | 164, overflow uncapped |

## How to choose

Choose the track where your team can produce the **strongest proof in eight hours**. Ask one question: *what can we realistically prove by 5 PM?*

**Choose Virality if:**
- Your team understands content, storytelling, or distribution.
- The product has a clear public sharing loop.
- You can drive impressions, visitors, signups, or meaningful actions.
- You already have access to an audience, community, or distribution channel.
- *Your proof: people discovered the product, shared it, visited it, and took action.*

**Choose Revenue if:**
- You understand a painful problem deeply.
- You know who the buyer is and how to reach them.
- The product can be used and purchased within the buildathon.
- Your team is strong at customer conversations, sales, product, execution.
- *Your proof: real users signed up, used the product, and paid real money.*

**Choose AI as Agency if:**
- Your team is strongest at engineering, agents, tools, workflow orchestration.
- You can replace a complete human function, not a single task.
- The agents can work across real tools and produce client-ready output.
- You can show the system completing work from brief to final delivery.
- *Your proof: a working team of agents completed a real job end to end.*

### Decision rule
- Can you create and distribute a story people will share? → **Virality.**
- Can you find a buyer and collect a payment? → **Revenue.**
- Can you build agents that complete a full business function? → **AI as Agency.**

Do **not** choose based on which track sounds most interesting. Choose based on where your team has the strongest advantage and can produce the clearest evidence by 5 PM.

## Cross-track bonus
You're scored on one track, but wins in someone else's track earn bonus points at **half weight, capped at 50 per team.** A Revenue build that also goes viral gets paid for it. Details on the [Scoring page](04-scoring.md#cross-track-bonus).
