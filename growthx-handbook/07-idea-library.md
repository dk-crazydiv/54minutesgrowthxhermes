**If you already have an idea, build that. If you don't, pick one from the library.**

## Bring your own idea or pick one of ours

- **Fresh builds only.** Any idea qualifies, not just this library. But it cannot be a product you were already working on: your company's product, your existing side project, anything with a head start does not apply. The build starts today.
- **It must live in one of the three tracks** — Virality, Revenue, or AI as Agency. You're judged on your track's rubric, with partner power-ups on top (see [Scoring](04-scoring.md)).

The library holds **93 ideas**, filterable by track (All / Virality / Revenue / AI as Agency) and sortable Easy → Hard.
