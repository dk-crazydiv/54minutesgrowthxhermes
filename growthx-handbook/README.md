## TL;DR — the day in one screen

- **One day, 8-hour build sprint.** 10 AM kickoff → 11 AM build → 5:30 PM live demos on stage.
- **You must use Hermes** (Nous Research's agent) in at least one of two ways — as your coding partner, or as the base harness your users interact with. **No Hermes, no score.** This is the *only* eligibility rule.
- **Pick one track** at registration — Virality, Revenue, or AI as Agency — and you're scored on that track's rubric only. Wins in other tracks earn a capped bonus.
- **Fresh builds only.** Built today, on the floor. No pre-existing or company products.
- **Numbers are verified, not trusted.** Live DB checks, analytics cross-checks, customer calls, bounce checks. A spoofed number zeroes that parameter.
- **Demo = 4 min on stage:** 2 min demo, 1 min proof (numbers on screen), 1 min Q&A. Live, not recorded.
- **Every accepted builder** walks away with ~$300+ in partner credits regardless of winning.
