### How to claim your perks

Each partner has its own flow. Most perks are tied to the email or org ID you registered with and **cannot be changed later**.

- **OpenAI — $200 credits + 1 month Codex Pro.** Granted automatically to the OpenAI org ID you submitted at registration. Codex Pro activates on your registered email, which must be linked to that org ID. Nothing to redeem. *No org ID at registration = no perk; it cannot be issued or reassigned later. This sits with OpenAI, not GrowthX.*
- **LinkUp — $50 in credits.** Sign up at LinkUp → Settings → **Add Credits** → select $50 and enter code **`HERMES`**.
- **Wispr Flow — 3 months free.** One link, no code entry. Sign up through it and the three months apply automatically.
- **ElevenLabs — 1 month of Creator.** Join the Discord → `#coupon-codes` channel → click **Start Redemption** → select event **GrowthX Hackathon** → fill the form with your registered email → the bot sends your unique coupon code.
- **Dodo Payments — zero fees on first $1,000.** Your unique code lands in your inbox (subject: *"Your Dodo Payments perk — GrowthX x Hermes Buildathon"*). Sign up → Settings → **Promotions** → enter your code.
