**Why GrowthX buildathons are built differently.**

- 🚀 **Everyone ships.** Everyone who walks in ships something. The whole day is built around getting you to a working demo.
- 👥 **The room.** The person next to you is a founder, engineer, or operator who ships. The room is half the value of the day.
- 🎯 **Build to win.** You get the exact scoring parameters before you write a line, so you build straight at what wins.
- 🔥 **Intensity.** The intensity comes from the room. This will be intense. That's the point. Trust us.

## Three things happen today

| Time | Phase | What's happening |
|------|-------|------------------|
| **10:00 AM** | Kickoff | Context, rules, the Hermes walkthrough. Then you pick your track and your idea. |
| **11:00 AM** | Build | The 8-hour sprint. Solo or teams. Build *with* Hermes or *on* Hermes, then spend the back half collecting proof. |
| **5:30 PM** | Demo | Final demos on stage. Two-minute demo, one-minute proof, one-minute Q&A. Live, not recorded. |
