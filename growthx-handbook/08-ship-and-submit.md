# 08 · Ship & submit

← [Index](README.md) | Prev: [Idea Library](07-idea-library.md) | Next: [Demo prep →](09-demo-prep.md)

**Others can use it, or it doesn't count.** Two things:

## 1. Ship to a real URL
Your build must be live somewhere another person can open and use it: a web app, a landing page, a bot link. **If a judge cannot use it from their own device, it does not count.**

## 2. Submit at `growthx.club/hermes-buildathon/submit`
Drop your live URL there before the deadline:

**→ <https://growthx.club/hermes-buildathon/submit>**

That is the only way to submit. No slides, no zip files.
