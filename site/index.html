Swiggy Companion Agent

Order from Swiggy without navigating Swiggy. A Hermes agent on Telegram that searches, repeats, optimises, places and tracks your Food and Instamart orders — you just chat.

Message the bot on Telegram Open the link, say hi, tell it what you're hungry for.

Why

Ordering food is nine steps: open the app, search, pick a restaurant, check delivery time, compare prices, fill the cart, hunt for coupons, check out, track. The agent turns that into one or two messages. The value isn't clever prediction — it's fewer clicks between you and a placed order.

You always give the final yes. The agent prepares carts; it never checks out on its own.

The five flows

1. Natural-language search "Find a vegetarian dinner under ₹350." No filters, no scrolling — describe what you want and pick from real options.
2. Repeat a previous order "Repeat yesterday's lunch." The agent reads your order history and rebuilds the cart in one message.
3. Cart optimise "You need ₹64 more for this coupon — but adding ₹90 only saves ₹50, so skip it." Coupon thresholds, minimum order value, free delivery, your budget.
4. Calendar-aware timing "Get lunch before my 1 PM meeting." The agent checks your calendar and finds food that lands before you're busy.
5. Back-in-stock watch "Tell me when this is back." The agent polls an unavailable Instamart product and pings you with a ready cart when it returns.

What it looks like

You: Get me lunch before my 1 PM meeting. Keep it under ₹300.

Agent: You have a meeting from 1 to 2. Three options that arrive before 12:45: South Indian meal ₹245, rice bowl ₹279, veg thali ₹290. Which one?

You: Rice bowl.

Agent: Added. Total ₹279. A coupon needs ₹349, but adding an item costs more than it saves. Proceed with ₹279?

You: Yes.

Agent: Ordered. I'll track it here and tell you when it's close.

How it works

You → Hermes companion agent on Telegram → Swiggy MCP → Food and Instamart. Google Calendar plugs in for timing. Back-in-stock is scheduled polling, since there's no webhook.

  • You approve every order before checkout. Always.
  • Prices get re-verified at cart confirmation — no stale search results.
  • Checkout never retries blindly, so no duplicate orders.
